Food For Thought: Mapping Hunger in Ontario

About client

Feed Ontario is the province's largest collective of hunger-relief organizations. Together with food banks, industry partners, and local communities, we work to end hunger and poverty by delivering fresh and healthy food, developing innovative programming, and driving change through research and advocacy.

Project Description

Our project's primary objective is to develop a user-friendly cartographic web product that corresponds with industry standards to illustrate how people utilize food banks and their socio-economic factors. We accomplished this aim by utilizing experience builder and web maps. In the web experince we incorperated pie charts and dynamic texts which will responds to the selected provincial riding on the web map to display the information regarding the selected feature.

Technologies used in this project

FME Workbench, Microsoft Access, ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, ArcGIS Experience Builder.
