ArcGIS Solutions

  • Phase 1: Experience Builder Exploration

    The team decided to deleate one (of four total) factors to each member. This was done to divide up the workload and allow each member to work on their own item and learn the platform at their own pace. Each member reviewed a tutorial for the Esri exeprience builder platform to allow for a quicker learning curve. Four separate experiences were created in experience builder, one for each user and factor. It was found that the experiences were not mobile friendly and did not have a common formatting theme.

  • Phase 2: Theme Application and Mobile Device Optimization

    To fix these issues in Phase 1, each team member was required to conform their experiences to the common formatting items that were decided upon. The experiences were also modified to be mobile friendly by each of the team members by viewing their experiences in mobile format (live view). After this phase was completed each of the experiences contained a common look and feel (theme) and worked on the mobile device format (Portrait).

  • Phase 3: Proposed Solution

    For the final part of this project, it was requied to bring all the components together under one shared experience. Each of the individual factors are able to be explored and simply toggled through by the user. This combined experience will simplify it for the user, allow a quicker uptake of the information, and make the overall experience more comprehensible.
